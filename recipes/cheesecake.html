cheescake

Description

This cherry cheesecake is a cross between a cherry cheesecake and a cherry cream cheese pie. It is delicious!

Ingredients

    1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
    1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
    â…“ cup lemon juice
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
    1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

How to make this cheescake

  • Step 1

    Place condensed milk, softened cream cheese, lemon juice, and vanilla into a mixing bowl; beat until well blended.

  • Step2

    Pour mixture into pie crust. Chill in the refrigerator for 5 hours; do not freeze.

  • Step 3

    Pour cherry pie filling on top of pie; serve.
